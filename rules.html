Server Rules

Welcome to DU! Please follow these guidelines to keep our developer community safe.

01

Respect Everyone

Be respectful to all members. No harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, or hate speech.

02

No Spamming

Do not spam messages, emojis, commands, or mention users repeatedly.

03

No Unrelated Advertising

Advertising other servers is strictly prohibited. Advertising Roblox games is allowed in designated areas.

04

No Malicious Links

Sending scams, phishing links, viruses, or suspicious downloads will result in an immediate ban.

05

Use Channels Correctly

Keep conversations in the correct channels and stay on topic to help others navigate.

06

No Drama or Toxicity

Do not start unnecessary arguments or create a toxic environment in the server.

07

Respect Staff

Listen to moderators and admins. Ignoring staff instructions may result in punishment.

08

No Exploiting

Do not abuse bots, roles, or server features. Report bugs to staff immediately.

09

Follow Discord ToS

Breaking Discord’s Terms of Service or Community Guidelines may lead to immediate removal.

Understood the rules?

By staying in the server, you agree to follow all the guidelines above.
